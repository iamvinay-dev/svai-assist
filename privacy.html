Privacy Policy

App Name: SVAI Assist

Last Updated: March 2026

1. About SVAI Assist

SVAI Assist is a WhatsApp chatbot built for students of S.V. Arts College (Autonomous), TTD, Tirupati. It helps BSc AI students with timetables, syllabus, exam schedules and subject doubts.

2. Data Collection

SVAI Assist does NOT collect, store or share any personal data of users. Messages sent to the bot are processed only to generate responses and are not stored anywhere.

3. Third Party Services

SVAI Assist uses Groq AI API only to generate responses to student queries. No personal information is shared with any third party.

4. Data Security

We do not store any user messages or personal information. All conversations are processed in real time and discarded immediately after response is sent.

5. Children's Privacy

SVAI Assist is designed for college students above 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
